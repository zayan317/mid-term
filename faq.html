Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account on your e-commerce app?

    To create an account, follow these steps:
  • Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the top-right corner of the homepage.
  • Fill out the required information, including your name, email address, and password.
  • Click "Register" or "Sign Up."
  • You'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the email to verify your account.
  • Congratulations! Your account is now created, and you can start shopping.
